FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! When you create a team, you automatically get a 14 day free trial in which you can cancel at any time without paying a dime.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely! We charge on a monthly basis because we know that most volleyball seasons are not year round. Therefore you should only pay for the months that you use the service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and process the payments through Stripe. You will get an invoice in your email for every payment we process.

What happens when I cancel?

Doctor Volleyball has two Modes: Inseason and Offseason. Changing your account to Offseason Mode stops all payments but prevents you from recording stats on new games. All your data is still available in offseason mode and will be indefinitely.
