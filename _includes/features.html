FEATURES

  • Record

Record stats with ease

Use a keyboard, or a touch screen to record stats.

Doctor Volleyball lets you record stats in two different ways. You can use the touch interface on a tablet or you can type the codes.

The touch interface is designed to be easy and quick to use, so you don't have to look down at the screen often.

The typing interface was created so you never had to look down at a screen. This allows you to keep your eyes on the rally the entire time.

Distribute with everyone

Have your athletes or assistant coaches sign up to see your team's stats.

Every member of your team can (and should) create an account and join your team. They will have access to all the stats you record the second you record them (if you have an internet connection).

Analyse your performance

View your stats in a variety of different ways

You can see your team's performance on the season to date, or on a select subset of the game that you have played.

Every match provides a breakdown by athlete, by set, and by position (what position the setter was on the court when that action happened).

Every athlete on your team has their own page (that anyone can see) where their stats are on display along with how they are trending over time.

You can optionally record stats on your opponents as you play them, and analyze your opponents stats in the exact same manner as your own.

Available anywhere

Built with the latest web technologies, Doctor Volleyball is available on every device.

Even though it is a humble website, DrVB can be added to the home screen of both iOS and Android devices and fully mimics a native application.

Any device you own that has access to the internet: has access to Doctor Volleyball.
